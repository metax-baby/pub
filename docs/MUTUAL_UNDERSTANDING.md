# Mutual Understanding

This document records current working meanings for PUNNARAJ. It is a living reference, not final truth.

## 1. Knowledge

Knowledge is not merely stored data. Knowledge is a reusable process or method that can be applied across changing data sets to solve problems.

Data may change. The method that continues to work across different cases is the knowledge.

## 2. Expertise

Expertise is a refined layer of knowledge produced by repeated use of a method. It is not final authority and not a god-state of knowledge.

Every expert method remains bounded by time, resources, technology, and context.

## 3. Wisdom

Wisdom is not the possession of many records. It is the ability to read, link, evaluate, compare, and decide whether a relation should become a method, remain a note, or require further inspection.

## 4. RAOS

RAOS is boundary-aware comparison.

A unit must know that its view is limited. It checks itself, checks the surrounding environment, compares internal understanding with external knowledge, and treats differences as inspection targets.

## 5. Human and AI responsibility

AI is strong at data structure, metadata, provenance, duplication, file relationships, ambiguity linking, and repetitive background operations.

Humans are responsible for real-world meaning, lived context, identity, intent, family correction, and final authority over sensitive interpretation.

## 6. Sensitive vs secret

Secret and sensitive are not the same.

Sensitive data is information whose disclosure can create disadvantage, leverage, harm, or loss of position. Sensitivity is judged by consequence and context, not by vocabulary alone.

## 7. ZERO / ALPHA / BETA

ZERO, ALPHA, and BETA are not rigid sacred zones. They are sensitivity and exposure-management layers.

The purpose is useful work with bounded blast radius, not paralysis from boundary fear.

## 8. Tool and service value

Every tool, service, account, domain, repository, machine, or payment path is a resource. If it consumes money, time, attention, complexity, storage, energy, or dependency, its value must be reviewed.

Cost must be justified by measurable improvement, not advertising language.

## 9. Template rule

Templates are useful because repeated structure helps humans and AI inspect, compare, link, and revise records.

Templates must start small and grow only when repeated friction proves a new field is necessary.

## 10. Minimum record

```yaml
id:
created:
```

`id` gives a record an address.

`created` gives it a place in history.

## 11. Historical honesty

The system is not a court. It does not need to judge the world.

It must preserve records honestly, including opinions, interpretations, later corrections, and the possibility that an earlier interpretation was wrong.

The goal is not to make the founder right forever. The goal is to preserve an inspectable history that future humans can review.
